  • Penghentian pemberian surat Notifikasi Submission

    2019-11-11

    Berdasarkan Keputusan Internal Editor JSAT mengenai surat Notifikasi Submission (Tanda Terima Naskah) untuk Author ditiadakan dan digantikan dengan Letter of Acceptance/LoA. LoA akan diberikan kepada Author apabila naskah tersebut sudah selesai melalui proses review oleh Mitra Bestari dan dinyatakan Diterima/Accepted oleh Editor in Chief. Surat Notifikasi Submission tidak lagi diberikan kepada Author. Atas perhatiannya kami mengucapkan terima kasih.

  • Call for Papers Vol. 3 (2019)

    2019-05-13

    Journal of Science and Applicative Technology (JSAT) is a national and international peer-review journal published by Lembaga Penelitian, Pengabdian Masyarakat, dan Penjaminan Mutu, Institut Teknologi Sumatera. JSAT is issued 2 times a year of electronic journal (July and December), JSAT publishes Original Research Articles (full papers and short communications) and Review (full and mini-reviews) in all aspects of result research in the field of Renewable Energy, Green Infrastructure, Community Development, Computer Science, Civil and Environmental Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Telecommunication, Earth Sciences (Geodesy, Geology, Geophysics, Astronomy), Natural Sciences (Mathematics, Chemistry, Physics, Statistics), Life Sciences (Biochemistry, Biology, Pharmacy), Regional and City Planning, Marine Engineering and Marine Science.
